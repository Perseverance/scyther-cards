## Notable Facts About Scyther Cards

1. **First Appearance**: Scyther debuted in the Jungle expansion (1999), making it one of the early Pok√©mon cards
2. **Print History**: The Jungle limited edition has known variants without the set symbol (error cards)
3. **Team Rocket Connection**: Rocket's Scyther is one of the few Pok√©mon with a dedicated Team Rocket variant
4. **EX Evolution**: Scyther was later printed as Scyther ex, and has a legal Scizor ex evolution
5. **Modern Reprints**: Scyther continues to be reprinted in nearly every modern set, keeping it readily available for collectors
6. **Language Variations**: Japanese releases often preceded English releases by several months
7. **Special Treatments**: Multiple secret rare and shiny variants demonstrate Scyther's popularity
