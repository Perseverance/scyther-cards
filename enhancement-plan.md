## Variation Rules by Era

### WOTC Era (1999-2003)
- **1st Edition**: Jungle, Neo Discovery, Neo Destiny, Gym Heroes, Aquapolis
- **Unlimited**: All WOTC sets
- **Shadowless**: Only Base Set (Scyther NOT in Base Set)

### e-Card Era (2002-2003)
- **Reverse Holo**: Aquapolis, Skyridge (but Scyther only in Aquapolis)

### EX Era (2003-2007)
- **Reverse Holo**: All main sets

### Diamond & Pearl through present
- **Reverse Holo**: Most main sets

## Known Scyther Errors

### Jungle #10/64 (Holo)
- **No Symbol Error**: Early Unlimited prints were printed without the Jungle set symbol
- Source: https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Scyther_(Jungle_10)
- Type: Uncorrected design error (corrected mid-print run)

- Jungle Scyther Non-Holo: https://images.pokemontcg.io/jungle/26_hires.png
