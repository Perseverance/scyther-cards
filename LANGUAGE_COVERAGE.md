## Promo Cards

Most promo cards (Wizards Promo 45, My First Battle, Start Deck 100, etc.) were released only in their respective regions:
- English promos → English only
- Japanese promos → Japanese only

## Summary Statistics

**Total New Cards to Add**: ~700-800 international variants
- Early sets (1999-2005): ~5-7 languages each = ~120-150 cards
- Mid-era (2008-2012): ~6 languages each = ~120 cards
- Modern era (2018-2024): ~10 languages each = ~400-500 cards

**Languages with Most Coverage**: 
1. English (all sets)
2. German (all major releases)
3. French (all major releases)
4. Italian (most major releases)
5. Spanish (most major releases since 2004)
6. Portuguese/Brazilian (2008+)
7. Korean (2018+)
8. Chinese Traditional & Simplified (2018+)
9. Thai (2018+)
10. Indonesian (2018+)

**Historical Languages**:
- Dutch: Only early sets (Jungle, Base Set 2)
- Russian: Very limited (Jungle only)
- Polish: Extremely limited
